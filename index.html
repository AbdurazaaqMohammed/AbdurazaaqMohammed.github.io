Projects

I have designed several Android applications, typically with a focus on compatibility with older versions to aid in maximizing the life of old devices and providing accessibility to those who cannot afford newer ones.

Many of these apps relate to my interest in reverse engineering and malware analysis of Android apps.

AntiSplit M

App to AntiSplit (merge) split APKs (APKS/XAPK/APKM) to regular .APK file on Android. Designed with a two versions; a Material You interface supporting Android 5.0+ and a legacy version for compatibility with Android 1.6+.

~

AXML Editor

Android app to edit android binary XML files (AndroidManifest.xml and layout XML files).

~

APK Extractor

Simple APK extractor app for Android supporting split APKs and saving them as single APK. Also supports every version of Android from 1.6+ and contains features for extracting specific files or attributes of apps such as icons.

~

APK Signer

Simple Android app to sign APKs, supports signing split APKs and multiple files at once.

~
